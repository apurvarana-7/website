About Me

Hi, I'm Apurva Rana, a digital marketing professional and content creator passionate about creating meaningful brand experiences through social media, visual storytelling, and creative strategy.

Through experience across social media marketing, content creation, influencer collaborations, SEO, email marketing, and digital campaigns, I enjoy combining creativity with data to create content that not only looks engaging but also supports brand growth. I have worked on developing social media strategies, creating branded graphics, producing short form videos, managing content calendars, and collaborating with brands to create authentic digital experiences.

My approach to marketing focuses on understanding audiences, creating content that tells a story, and using insights to continuously improve performance. From designing social media visuals and editing reels to analyzing engagement trends and identifying opportunities for growth, I enjoy every step of the creative process.

I have experience creating content across platforms including Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, Pinterest, and Facebook, while using tools such as Canva, Adobe Creative Suite, CapCut, Meta Insights, GA4, and marketing analytics platforms to bring ideas to life and measure impact.

This portfolio showcases my creative journey through social media designs, reels, brand collaborations, and personal content creation. Each project reflects my passion for storytelling, branding, and building digital connections that help brands stand out in today's evolving online landscape.
